Why Explainable AI Matters: Building Trust in Intelligent Systems

2025-11-11 8 min
As AI systems become more prevalent in critical decision-making processes, the need for explainability has never been greater. Black-box models, while powerful, can erode trust and raise ethical concerns.</p><p>In my research work on AI-powered Python debugging, implementing Explainable AI (XAI) techniques like LIME and SHAP transformed user engagement. Students could see why the AI suggested certain fixes, which not only built trust but enhanced learning outcomes.</p><p>The future of AI isn't just about accuracy—it's about transparency, accountability, and building systems that humans can understand and trust. This is especially crucial in education, healthcare, and finance where decisions have real-world consequences.
