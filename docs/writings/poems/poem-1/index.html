Digital Dreams

2025-11-12 2 min
Lines of code, like verses flow, In Python's grace, ideas grow. Algorithms dance, patterns gleam, Building tomorrow's digital dream. Data whispers tales untold, In neural nets, futures unfold. From zero, one, worlds we create, Where innovation meets its fate. Debug the heart, refine the mind, In every error, lessons find. For code is art, and art is code, On this eternal learning road.
