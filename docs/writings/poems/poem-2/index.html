The Learner's Journey

2025-11-09 2 min
Each day a page, each moment ink, In books of life, we pause and think. Questions rise like morning sun, Knowledge sought, never done. From classroom walls to real-world tests, We grow through failures, find our bests. Every challenge, every fall, Teaches us to stand more tall. So let us learn, forever more, Open every knowledge door. For wisdom comes not from one place, But from life's continuous embrace.
