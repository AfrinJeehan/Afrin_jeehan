Time Management for Student Developers and Researchers

2025-11-07 5 min
**1. Use Time Blocking**: Dedicate specific hours to specific tasks. No multitasking during these blocks.</p><p>**2. Prioritize with Impact Matrix**: Urgent AND Important gets done first. Important but not urgent gets scheduled.</p><p>**3. Learn to Say No**: Not every opportunity is right for you right now. Focus on alignment with your goals.</p><p>**4. Batch Similar Tasks**: Code all at once, write all at once, read papers all at once. Context switching kills productivity.</p><p>**5. Rest is Productive**: Your brain needs downtime. Schedule breaks and actually take them.
