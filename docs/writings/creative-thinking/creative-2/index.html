The Art of Asking Better Questions

2025-11-03 5 min
The quality of your solutions depends on the quality of your questions. Here's how to ask better ones:</p><p>**Instead of**: 'How can I make this faster?'<br>**Ask**: 'What's the real bottleneck here? Is speed even the right metric?'</p><p>**Instead of**: 'What technology should I use?'<br>**Ask**: 'What problem am I actually solving? Who benefits? What constraints matter most?'</p><p>**Instead of**: 'How do I fix this bug?'<br>**Ask**: 'Why did this bug occur? What systemic issue does it reveal?'</p><p>Good questions open possibilities. Great questions challenge assumptions. Start questioning your questions.
