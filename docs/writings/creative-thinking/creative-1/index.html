From Problem to Solution: A Creative Framework

2025-11-10 7 min
Creative problem-solving isn't magic—it's a process. Here's my framework:</p><p>**1. Reframe the Problem**: Don't accept the problem as stated. Ask 'why' five times to get to the root cause.</p><p>**2. Gather Diverse Inputs**: Read outside your field. Talk to people with different backgrounds. Inspiration comes from unexpected places.</p><p>**3. Generate Abundantly**: Quantity before quality. List 20 solutions, no matter how crazy. You can filter later.</p><p>**4. Combine Ideas**: The best solutions often merge multiple concepts. Mix and match your ideas.</p><p>**5. Prototype Quickly**: Build a minimum viable version. Learn from failure fast.</p><p>When I developed the Share Plate platform, we reframed 'food waste' as 'resource optimization,' which opened up entirely new solution paths.
